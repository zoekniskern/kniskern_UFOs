UFO Sightings Around the World

Historically, the subject of UFOs or Unidentified Flying Objects, has been laughed at as the overexcitement of average joes at the sight of anything remotely unfamiliar in the sky. However, in an age where space travel is longer unthinkable and technology has advanced significantly, are UFOs back on the academic menu?

Many civilian groups have formed data collectives to track and study UFO sightings, even a group of 60 masters students examined UFO documentation for a data science project!

While it seems that reported sightings have decreaesed, potentially due to a lack of interest, a recent Defense Intelligence Agency briefing suggests that Pentagon's long alleged UFO project is still operational.

The UFO & US Love Affair

One common critique of UFO sighting data, is that a majority of it comes from the US. As you can see in the bar chart, this is undisputably true of this dataset. This could be attributed to an american affinity to science fiction and country-wide culture or interest in extra terrestrial life!

Some scientists urge people to look past the UFO track record, arguing that although many of the reported sightings may not be extra terrestrial, the existance other life forms in this universe is still highly likely!
